About#

  • Why: Tectonically uplifted limestone karst; similar geology to adjacent parts of Indonesia.
  • Status: Limited cave documentation; no cave diving.
  • Potential: First exploration of resurgence systems and flooded karst valleys.
  • Best Season: May to October. Best month = September

  • Lautém Municipality: Lene Hara Cave, Jerimalai, Ile Kére Kére

  • Manatuto District: Laili
  • Bobonaro District: Marobo Hot Springs
