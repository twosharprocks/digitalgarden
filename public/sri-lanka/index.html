About#

Central Highlands Karst

  • Why: Significant limestone in the Central and Northern provinces, including areas near Dambulla.
  • Status: Largely undocumented in terms of cave exploration, but karst water systems are present.
  • Potential: First cave diving in small flooded sinkholes or phreatic systems during wet season.
  • Best Season: January to March (dry season for access).
